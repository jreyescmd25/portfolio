Details about the Game

Overview

Mango comes from the heavens and reaches out to Bitterberry to recruit David in order to rescue his cousin Jason, who is held captive by Emperor Bro. Along the way, Players must defeat several species of mutant birds particularly cockatiels possessed by his minions. It’s up to David, Bitterberry, and Bitterberry’s six children to stop the world from falling into mass havoc.

Players take control of David and explore locations such as SoHo, The Wild Cuckling West, Emperor Bro’s fortress, and a special post-game location unlocked after completing the base game. As players progress, they unlock abilities that expand their combat options, allowing them to shoot, punch, and customize their playstyle. Do you have what it takes to defeat Emperor Bro?

Core Concept

An action-adventure, dialogue-driven, 2D Platformer Game with an engaging, humorous, and emotional-driven story made up of memorable characters. The game primarly focuses on completing levels within 12 minuete time constrains, having the ability to change the player's kit for more options, obtaining collectables, and finding secrets.

Characters

Playable Characters:

David

Known to be main protagonist of the game, has life mostly figured out, wants to be just like one of his High School instructors; however, he’s not quite there. He’s got a lot of connections that he barely has enough time for himself let alone with his cousin. He is also notorious for leaving his besties on delivered, Jason being included

Jason

Known to be the unlockable character, still figuring out life, takes inspiration from many of his major influences. He is not as open as David is, since he values alone time, however, he's still open to those who demonstrate loyalty, but surely, he won't leave you on delivered let alone on read .

Main Non-Playable Characters:

Mango

The one and only Angelic Birb from the heavens here to alert Bitterberry to save the world from Emperor Bro’s Destruction She was presented with a future that was presented by the heavens that frightened her. It involved her loved ones back on earth, ofc her owner and his family. She saw how Emperor Bro would ultimately finish humanity and other species by turning them into abominations and ultimately getting rid of every organism that exists, thus without hesitation she asks for the next steps where this one female (Perlimon) tells her to find a specific depressed bird (her husband) to help along the way to find the right guy for the job so that he can rescue her owner so they can team up to finish him off.

Bitterberry

Known to be quite the bitter one, however he was not always like this. Matter a fact, he was known to be incredibly happy and patient since he had a beloved wife on his side, going on adventures, sharing and celebrating the most life and sharing family time with the kids. Until one day, his wife went missing and lost her around a waterfall, when being attacked by a flock of large eagles, hence why he hates the statues David obtains throughout their missions.

Emperor Bro

The Main Antagonist himself, yes, he’s wicked, yes, he hates you, yes, he wants to rule the universe, took possession of an innocent cockatiel and human being fused them together to make what is Emperor Bro’s final form, why is he named Emperor Bro? He does not necessarily know English when he saw a message on the guy’s phone and saw a text saying ”bro” he thought it was cool. He originates in Kepler-b420 where he plans to share his power with the rest of his planet to ultimately rule the universe

Main Antagonists:

DJ Funkingo

Known as the first boss of the game, notorious for playing records, however he does not like humans, even him being a mix of a human and a flamingo. He does not like Emperor Bro as much, but was down to take care of business once Emperor Bro told vlud that he’d get some records as a reward.

Don Ladron

Known as The Wild Clukin’ West’s most wanted criminal, he’s a pro when it comes to hiding his identity, many still believe he’s dead, but he’s always out there having a slugfest whenever it gets too quiet. He felt like it was an amazing time to strike when Emperor Bro was unleashing his Havoc and of course when it comes to treasure, he’s out his ears, nose, and eyes wide open ready to trade for some quick cash !

Current Development Status

As of now, Project: Codename Yessir is in an early prototype stage of development. The core structure, narrative flow, UI layouts, and overall game concept have been fully designed and documented. A playable prototype is currently available to download, featuring implemented dialogue scenes, a functional title screen, and core presentation systems. Development is actively progressing toward player-controlled gameplay, with the next major milestone focusing on enabling the player to take control of David following the opening dialogue sequences. This project currently is primarily a solo-developed effort, with additional support from collaborators who contributed voice acting for select scenes.
